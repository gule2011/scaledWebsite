Height Safety Certifications

The team at Scaled are skilled proffesionals who are trained in assessing the functionality and quality of your height safety.

When Scaled perform a anchor certification, we not only test, tag and provide a detailed report of all your height safety systems but we will also perform a full audit of your heights working area and give recommendations if anything could be improved or is not in compliance with legislation.

Design & Installations

Scaled has a team with extensive practical experience with working at heights as well as the knowledge to comply with all of the legislative requirements. We can design and install systems for all things heights safety, whether its for absieling to clean windows, fall arrest for gutter cleaning or you just need walkway installed to avoid slips and trips.

Equipment Certifications

Using height safety equipment requires inspections and certifications, usually once every 6 months to ensure it's going to work when you need it the most. Thats why Scaled employees are all trained to inspect and certify height safety equipment.

We know it can be a hassle to take all your equipment in to be certified, thats why Scaled do site visits.
